About Me

I am currently a Kruskal instructor in the Department of Statistics, University of Chicago. I completed my PhD from the Department of Statistics and Data Science at University of Pennsylvania. I was jointly advised by Prof. Zongming Ma and Prof. Bhaswar B. Bhattacharya. I did my Bachelors and Masters in Statistics from Indian Statistical Institute, Kolkata.

Research

My research interests include high dimensional statistics; integration, alignment, and visualization of multi-omic single-cell data; graphical models; random matrix theory; and non-parametric statistics. My publications and preprints can be found here.
